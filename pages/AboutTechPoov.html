About TechPoov

TechPoov is an engineering-driven initiative built on more than 25 years of real-world IT experience across multiple countries, industries, and technology stacks.

The mission is simple: create small, reliable tools that solve the everyday problems people face. No heavy software. No complicated setup. Just practical utilities that remove friction from your digital life.

TechPoov tools focus on addressing common challenges in:

  • Google Drive organization and deep-folder analysis
  • Backup and replication workflows
  • Windows filesystem scanning and reporting
  • Metadata extraction and compliance support
  • Automation for repetitive, error-prone tasks

Every tool is designed around three core principles:

  • Simplicity: Minimal configuration, no installation, intuitive output.
  • Reliability: Handles large datasets, resumes safely, produces clean results.
  • Practical Value: Inspired directly by everyday IT pain points.

TechPoov continues to grow, but the philosophy remains the same: build tools that save time, reduce stress, and bring clarity to complex digital environments.
